Nutella Pancakes


2 sevings
45 minutes


INGREDIENTS


100 ml milk
50 g butter
3 eggs
1 tbs cocoa
2 tsp baking soda
a pinch of salt
3 eggs

DIRECTIONS


1. Whisk together the dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.
2. Mix the melted butter with the Nutella and then mix in the egg and milk.
3. Add the Nutella mixture to the dry ingredients and mix till smooth.
4. Pour about Â¼ cup of the batter per pancake on a preheated and greased electric griddle or griddle pan.
5. Cook for about 45-60 seconds on the first side until they start to brown and bubbles form on the top.
6. Flip them over to cook for an additional 30 seconds until golden browned.
7. Top the finished Nutella pancakes with chopped hazelnuts and maple syrup!
